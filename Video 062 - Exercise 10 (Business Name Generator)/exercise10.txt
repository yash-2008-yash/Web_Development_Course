Create a Business Name Generator with JavaScript.

ADJECTIVES: Crazy, Amazing, Fire
SHOP NAMES: Engine, Foods, Garments
ANOTHER WORD: Bros, Limited, Hub

You should take each word from these 3 sections and merge it to a business name.
For example, Crazy Foods Hub, Fire Engine Bros, etc.

It should create a random business name everytime executed.

NOTE: You should not use Arrays to solve this exercise.
